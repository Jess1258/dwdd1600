The Insider

Learn about the Disadvantages and Advantages of taking on the role of the oldest sibling. There are many reasons why the oldest is shaped into the person they are and below or the few examples as to why.

Sometimes, I think about what it would be like if I wasn't an First Born Child. There are many experiences comapring to power and compramise that one has with being the oldest sibiling. As I got oldere I realized that there were many disadvantages and advantages to being an older sibling and many of them made me who I am today. Comparing to my own experience here are some examples I resonated too.

Disadvantages for being the Oldest

  1. You're always the responsible one
  2. You still are a child, regardless of the amount of responsibilities you have
  3. You can become a bit of a Ham
  4. Leadership can turn into bossiness...

Advantages for being the Oldest

  1. There's no bar set by older siblings or cousins
  2. You learn leadership skills every single day
  3. You're the first to do everything!
  4. You set the trends!
